The idea is that we want to minimize the amount of coins from `a` and that we maximize the value we have. 
From the example where there are only 2 coins both equal to 3, we end up taking all of the coins since the only criteria that needs to be satisifed is that the sum of our coins are stricltly larger. if we were to split it up into 3 and 3, this does not satisied the requirement.
